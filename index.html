Weighback™

Your weight, in context.

A quiet companion for the weight history Apple Health already has. Weighback finds the patterns no scale alone can show — milestones, peaks, and the last time you weighed what you weigh today.

What it does

  • Weighback. Tells you the last time you weighed your current weight. Sometimes the number on the scale feels familiar — Weighback tells you exactly when you last saw it.
  • Milestones. A descending chain of every meaningful low you've crossed, with the dates you reached them.
  • All-time low and peak, always one tap away.
  • A chart that zooms. One month to full history, with the same kind of momentum panning you expect from Apple's own apps.

What it doesn't do

  • Send your data anywhere. Weighback runs entirely on your iPhone. Your weight history stays where it already is — in Apple Health, on your device.
  • Ask you to log in. There is no account. There is no server.
  • Charge to unlock features. The app is free. A small in-app donation supports development; the feature set is the same either way.

Get it

Weighback is available on the App Store for iPhone and iPad, running iOS 17 or later.
